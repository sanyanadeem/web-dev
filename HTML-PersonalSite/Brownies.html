How to Bake Ghirardelli Triple Chocolate Chip Brownies:

An original recipe from Sanya Nadeem.

Yum! Just thinking about these brownies makes me lick my lips. These brownies are made from a mix by a decadent chocolate company--yep, you guessed it, Ghirardelli! These brownies have three types of chocolate chips in them, so you can keep getting that fudgy goodness in each bite! Savor this brownie, and serve with cool vanilla ice cream, caramel syrup, or without any topping at all! No matter which way you eat these desserts, you'll for sure find yourself making these over and over again!

Ingredients: (makes 16 brownies)

  • 1 pouch Ghirardelli Triple Chocolate Brownie Mix
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • P.S: To make these brownies even tastier, you can add some crushed Oreos as well!

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F and grease an 11" by 7" pan.

  2. Blend water, oil and egg(s) in the mixing bowl. Add brownie mix and stir until moistened. Spoon batter into prepared pan and spread evenly.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven. Add 5 minutes to the baking time if using a glass pan. Refer to the table below for total baking time. Now it's time to enjoy this delight!
Number of Brownies: Baking Time:
16 brownies 45-50 minutes
24 brownies 50-55 minutes
48 brownies 45-50 minutes
